About this spot

Plage d'Albigny is arguably the most beautiful and largest beach on the shores of Lake Annecy. Known for its crystal-clear turquoise waters and the stunning backdrop of the French Alps, it offers a perfect mix of sandy areas and lush green lawns.

The beach is free to access and particularly popular with families due to its shallow entry points. During the summer months, the water temperature reaches a pleasant 22-24°C.
